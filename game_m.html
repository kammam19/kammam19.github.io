Game : Oh No! The Cat drop my pictures.

Game Story

At Kammam's gallery

Today little cat came in the gallery.

And then the little cat stole the picture.

No one knows why it was stolen.

And now that we know where the pictures are hidden, please help we get them back.

How to play

This is section on the game play page.

1. Click button play to start game.

2. Timer start countdown.

3. Pick up as many pictures as you can until the time is up.

4. When time up, you will see amount of pictures.
